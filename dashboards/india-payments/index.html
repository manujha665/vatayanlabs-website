Key Highlights

UPI is the silent disruptor behind every trend in this dataset. The RBI's ATM & Card Statistics captures UPI infrastructure (QR codes, PoS terminals) but not UPI transaction volumes directly — those sit in NPCI data. What we see here are the effects of UPI: debit card PoS death, ATM rationalisation, and the explosion of acceptance infrastructure.
